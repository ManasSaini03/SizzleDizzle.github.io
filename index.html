Instructions

  • The players navigate their counters from left to right, starting at 1, following the numbers on the board, then the next row from right to left and repeat.
  • If a player lands on the bottom of a ladder / Head of Snake, a question will appear.
  • If the player answers the question correctly , their counter climbs up to the square at the top end of ladder.
  • But if the player answers the quesion wrong , their counter slides down to square at the snake's tail.
  • To win the game, player must reach the highest numbered square(100)
